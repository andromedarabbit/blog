Someone on [quora]() asked:
> [Would you rather be confident or be perceived as confident?](https://www.quora.com/Would-you-rather-be-confident-or-be-perceived-as-confident/answer/Roy-Tang)
<span class="ui_qtext_rendered_qtext"><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">In the long run, these will tend to be the same. If people perceive you as confident, it tends to create a positive feedback loop that soon enough you *will* be confident</p></span>
