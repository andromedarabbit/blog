Someone on [quora]() asked:
> [What's the fastest way to improve someone's day?](https://www.quora.com/Whats-the-fastest-way-to-improve-someones-day/answer/Roy-Tang)
<span class="ui_qtext_rendered_qtext"><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">1. Compliment them unexpectedly on their appearance or their work</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">2. Surprise treats like a donut or a cookie</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">3. Smile and say hi to a stranger, you'd be surprised how a simple thing can turn around someone's day</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">4. Send thank you notes</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">5. Pay for the next guy's coffee</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">6. Let the next person ahead of you in line at the grocery store</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">7. Text an old friend you haven't spoken to in a while</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">8. Write a short poem</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">9. Free hugs!</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">10. On your way to the store, ask your neighbor or office mate if they want anything</p></span>
