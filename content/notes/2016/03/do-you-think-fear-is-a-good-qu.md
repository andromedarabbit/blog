Someone on [quora]() asked:
> [Do you think fear is a good quality?](https://www.quora.com/Do-you-think-fear-is-a-good-quality/answer/Roy-Tang)
<span class="ui_qtext_rendered_qtext"><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Fear can keep you alive, that in itself makes it a good quality. But you have to be able not to let fear control you, to recognize when the fear you're having is irrational.</p></span>
