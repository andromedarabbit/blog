Someone on [quora](https://quora.com) asked:
> [I am bored and I really love writing so I want to write a short story. What are some good Topics?](https://www.quora.com/I-am-bored-and-I-really-love-writing-so-I-want-to-write-a-short-story-What-are-some-good-Topics/answer/Roy-Tang)
<span class="ui_qtext_rendered_qtext"><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Write about your city, about something that can only happen there.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Write about someone's first love. And how it was lost.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Write about someone who has a friend who's in trouble. Accused of something bad. And eventually redeemed.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Write about a tourist, lost in a strange city after having taken the wrong bus.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Write about a woman who meets a stranger in a restaurant, and he tells her something that changes her life forever.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Write about a man on his deathbed, confessing something to his son, about an event that happened three decades ago.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Write about a hunter stalking his prey, only to realize his prey was wilier than he thought, and that soon, he would become the prey.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Write about a falling star, and how it changes the lives of the people in a small town</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Write about a criminal who victimizes someone he shouldn't have and lives to regret it</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Write about a man living a lie, hiding from his past, and how it eventually catches up with him.</p></span>
