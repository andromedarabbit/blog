> [How can I become less bored with life?](https://www.quora.com/How-can-I-become-less-bored-with-life/answer/Roy-Tang)

What is something you always wanted to try?</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">3. Start looking for a new job.
