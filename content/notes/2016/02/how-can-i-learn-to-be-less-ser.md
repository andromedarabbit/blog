Someone on [quora]() asked:
> [How can I learn to be less serious when the world seems to me a very serious place?](https://www.quora.com/How-can-I-learn-to-be-less-serious-when-the-world-seems-to-me-a-very-serious-place/answer/Roy-Tang)
<span class="ui_qtext_rendered_qtext"><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Spend time with young kids, age 6-10. And don't be shy or reluctant if they ask you to do silly things or play games - the point is to let go and enjoy life from a little kid's perspective</p></span>
