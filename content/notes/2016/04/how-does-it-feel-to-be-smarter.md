Someone on [quora]() asked:
> [How does it feel to be smarter than average?](https://www.quora.com/How-does-it-feel-to-be-smarter-than-average/answer/Roy-Tang)
<span class="ui_qtext_rendered_qtext"><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Depends on the crowd. Sometimes you'll feel out of place. You have to resist the urge to correct people all the time (many people don't like it). You have to avoid sounding like a smartass. You have to know when it is appropriate to use your smarts.</p></span>
