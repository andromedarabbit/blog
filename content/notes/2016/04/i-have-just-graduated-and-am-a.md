Someone on [quora]() asked:
> [I have just graduated and am a few months into my career. What tips can you give me that will help me improve in every aspect of my life?](https://www.quora.com/I-have-just-graduated-and-am-a-few-months-into-my-career-What-tips-can-you-give-me-that-will-help-me-improve-in-every-aspect-of-my-life/answer/Roy-Tang)
<span class="ui_qtext_rendered_qtext"><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Don't waste your time worrying about people who aren't helping you grow.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Develop an attitude towards learning and discovering new things.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Be kind and understanding to people, even assholes.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Every so often, walk down an unfamiliar road.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Tell your family and friends how much you appreciate them.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Exercise at least a little bit every day.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Try to improve some aspect of your life by 1% every day.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Thank people who help you and help others in return.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Don't be seduced by pretty appearances or the lure of easy money.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Don't insist on helping people who don't want to be helped.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Don't compromise yourself for the sake of other people.</p></span>
