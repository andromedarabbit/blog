Someone on [quora](https://quora.com) asked:

> [Is it to late for a 33 years old man become a software engineer?](https://www.quora.com/Is-it-to-late-for-a-33-years-old-man-become-a-software-engineer/answer/Roy-Tang)


<span class="ui_qtext_rendered_qtext"><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">It's never too late to be who you were meant to be.</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">That being said, it will be a bit more difficult to get into the industry the traditional way (companies are not likely to hire you). You will more likely be able to find work on a freelance basis.</p></span>
