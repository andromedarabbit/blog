The ally-color triples ("shards") such as UGW ("Bant") are from the Alara block. The enemy-color triples ("wedges") such as URG ("Temur") are from the Tarkir block. The 4-color ones are named after a cycle of 4-color creatures called Nephilim from the first Ravnica block.
