> Bring me all of your dreams,
> 
> You dreamer,
> 
> Bring me all your
> 
> Heart melodies
> 
> That I may wrap them
> 
> In a blue cloud-cloth
> 
> Away from the too-rough fingers
> 
> Of the world. 
> 
> - Langston Hughes
